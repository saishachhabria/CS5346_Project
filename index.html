The rise in migration across regions

Migration has gained prominence in recent years, and this trend can be seen in all the 4 different regions. Immigration to different regions has been rising since the 1990s, albeit with different rates across continents. This trend is prominent in Asia with a steep rise in number of immigrants as well as emigrants. In Europe there is only ~30% increase in emigrants in last 30 years, which highlights that people in Europe may not generally look out for better opportunities.

Distinguishable immigrant-emigrant trends in various continents

Migration patterns are not uniform and vary across regions. The pre-existing gap between the number of emigrants and immigrants in America has further widened in the last 5 years. Contrary to this, the difference in trends of immigrants and emigrants is low in Asia.
